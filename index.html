PLL Algorithms

This site is about PLL algorithms. PLL, or in short permutation of the last layer is the last step to solve the Rubik's Cube using the CFOP, or Fridich method. There are a total of 21 different cases, but in this site, we would be looking at a few different PLL cases. Let's get started!
